About Me

Hello! I'm Amzat, an aspiring web developer. I am 21 years old and I am in my final year of studying Chemical Engineering in the university. Sometime ago, I developed an interest in blockchain technology. Being someone who had never even written a line of code, getting into tech seemed like an impossible task, but I was determined. I have now spent the last six months learning frontend development as a first step to becoming a web developer. I have now learnt HTML, CSS, and I am currently learning Javascript.

I am a very focused and goal-oriented person and I always strive to achieve the best in whatever I do. This is the reason why, even though my coding journey has been somewhat tough for many reasons, I am not going to back down from this. I am going to keep on keeping on and learning everything I can and gradually I will get better at this. Someday, I'll look back at this website and be grateful for how far I have come and how much better I would have gotten.

Along my learning path, I was required to build some interesting projects. I made this website so I can show you some of those projects. I hope you like them!
